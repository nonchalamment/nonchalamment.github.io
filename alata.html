Alata







Alata

is a performance piece based on the eponymous instrument worn on the backs of the performers that models after bone deformities. The performer is unable to interact with the instrument due to its positioning on the back and requires another person in order to interact with it at all. The piece plays with ideas of empathy, acceptance, and the abject.
