Rose Spiral (2018)

Rose Spiral

is a solo full-length album (64') self-released under the pseudonym Eiyoun. The tracks are a product of an improvisational process using feedback systems. The album was recorded with the Magic Echo Music synthesizer (created by Kaori Suzuki) and homemade electronics consisting of an and-gate distortion circuit.
