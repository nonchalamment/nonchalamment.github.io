Medium Transparency



In

Medium Transparency,

an indeterminate number of performers interpret a composite graphic score assembled by another performer, designated as the score-person. The score elements are printed on transparencies which then are projected onto a surface, using either an overhead projector as pictured (black and white) or a digital projector (color). Throughout the piece, the score-person continuously redefine score through adding and subtracting score elements in various configurations.
